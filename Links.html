The Essentials

This part of the list contains the most useful links/tools I’ve found.

Ublock Origin - Free ad blocker, available on most browsers (although you should switch to Firefox if you haven’t already) Honestly without this, browsing the internet is absolutely insufferable.

FMHY - A huge list of free software, websites, and services for literally anything you could think of.

Sponsor Skip - Skips youtube sponsors, if you don’t use a youtube frontend with this installed already, I highly recommend it.

Marginalia Search - Search engine for personal blogs, I’ve found a lot of really interesting and charming websites through this.

Archive Today – News from reputable sources is hard to come by, and its a shame so much of it is locked behind a paywall, this extension allows you to view full articles that have been archived. Works on most news sites with paywalls.

Lightshot – simple screenshoting tool for win and mac.
